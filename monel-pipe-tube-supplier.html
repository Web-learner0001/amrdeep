What is a Monel pipe?

Monel Pipes have a higher tensile strength and tend to be stronger than pure nickel. ASME SB725 Monel Alloy Welded Pipes can handle sea water and steam well, even when the temperatures are high. In fact, the ASTM B165 Monel Seamless Pipe is resistant to corrosion by a wide range of corrosive media, including fast-moving seawater.

Monel 400 Pipe is made out of a nickel-copper alloy. This particular material is very strong, doesn't rust, and wears and tears very little. Steel India Company sells these pipes, and our customers like them because they are easy to bend and because they don't react with acids. The Monel 400 Pipe Price can change depending on the nominal size, the schedule, the wall thickness, the grade, and the application requirements. We make these pipes in three different ways: welded, electric resistance welded, and seamless.

The Monel 400 Seamless Pipe has exact dimensions. This level of accuracy is helpful for applications that need to be exact. All of our types of Alloy 400 Pipe are of high quality and have been tested to meet international standards. As one of the top Alloy 400 Pipe Suppliers, we also make these pipes in different nominal sizes and shapes. Most of the time, round pipes are used, but you can also get oval, rectangular, and square pipes in the ASTM B725 UNS N04400 grade. These pipes are used in places where temperatures range from below freezing to more than 500 degrees Celsius. We have more customers and are ready to take your orders for SB 165 UNS N04400 pipe products.
