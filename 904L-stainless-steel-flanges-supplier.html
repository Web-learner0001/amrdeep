In SS 904L Flanges, other things are also taken into account, like measurements, shapes, sizes, length, and durability. Flanges also come in aluminum, copper, polypropylene, and other materials. It also keeps the metal from oxidizing. These metals give it a shiny finish and make it work well. Flanges are made of stainless steel to protect them from different weather conditions. Also, there are different grades of flanges, such as stainless steel grades, copper grades, duplex grades, nickel alloy grades, and so on.

The SS 904L Plate Flange has a plate attached to it so that welding, bolting, and other ways of attaching can be done with more room. The flanges are made to ASTM B649 standards, which have different standards for ANSI, ASME, DIN, and EN. These flanges range in size from 1/2 inch to 48 inches, and the pressure classes are different for each size. Weld neck raised face flanges are what ASME SA182 SS 904L WNRF Flanges are. These have welded necks and raised faces to make them stronger. The pressure classes for these flanges range from 150 to 2500 and from PN6 to PN64. These flanges are used in many different fields, such as the pharmaceutical, petrochemical, marine, sea water, and power generation industries. The SS DIN 1.4539 Nippo Flanges are used in heat exchangers, boilers, condensers, the paper and pulp industry, offshore oil drilling, and the production of equipment for handling chemicals and sea water.
