Super Duplex Steel 2507 (UNS S32750) Pipes and Tubes Supplier & Exporter

super duplex 2507, Super Duplex Steel DIN 1.4410 Pipes & Tubes, UNS S32750 Seamless Pipes & Tubes Manufacturer & Exporter, Super Duplex Steel UNS S32750 Welded Pipes, UNS S32760 ERW Pipes, UNS S32750 Pipes Suppliers & Exporter

Amardeep Steel is a leading supplier and exporter of Super Duplex Steel 2507 (UNS S32750) and Super Duplex Steel UNS S32760 Pipes and Tubes. These products are made with high-quality raw materials that meet national and international standards, which makes them durable and high-quality. It is a 50/50 mix of austenite and ferrite, making it a super duplex stainless steel. Super Duplex Steel UNS S23750 Pipes and Tubes are made of a material that has high mechanical strength, good ductility, and good corrosion resistance in a number of different environments. Pipes and tubes made of Super Duplex Steel UNS S32760 are often used to make parts for industries like chemical processing, marine, oil and gas, pollution control, and food.

Corrosion Resistance

Duplex 2507 stainless steel (UNS S23750) is made with a lot of chromium and molybdenum and is very resistant to corrosion by organic acids. Duplex 2507 stainless steel (UNS S23750) has a high resistance to corrosion from inorganic acids as well. When sulfuric acid is mixed with chloride ions, duplex 2507 stainless steel (UNS S23750) is more resistant to corrosion than 904L stainless steel. 904L is an austenitic alloy that is used to protect against pure sulfuric acid corrosion.
